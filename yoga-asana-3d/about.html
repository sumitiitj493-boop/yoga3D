🧘 About Yoga 3D

Welcome to Yoga 3D, your comprehensive platform for learning and practicing yoga through interactive 3D visualization. Our mission is to make yoga accessible to everyone by combining ancient wisdom with modern technology.

✨ Why Yoga 3D?

Traditional yoga instruction can sometimes be challenging to follow, especially for beginners. Yoga 3D solves this problem by providing 360-degree, interactive 3D models of each pose, allowing you to see every angle and detail. Whether you're a complete beginner or an advanced practitioner, our platform helps you perfect your form and deepen your practice.

🎓 What You'll Learn

Our comprehensive library includes poses for all skill levels:

  • Beginner Poses: Perfect for those just starting their yoga journey
  • Intermediate Poses: Build strength, flexibility, and balance
  • Advanced Poses: Challenge yourself with complex asanas

💡 How It Works

1. Create an Account: Sign up to access all features including progress tracking and personalized recommendations.
2. Explore Poses: Browse our gallery or use the 3D viewer to explore different asanas.
3. Practice: Use the built-in timer to track your practice sessions.
4. Track Progress: View your dashboard to see your improvement and celebrate milestones.
5. Stay Consistent: Build streaks and unlock achievements as you develop a regular practice.

🌟 Technology Stack

Yoga 3D is built with cutting-edge web technologies:

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Three.js for 3D rendering
  • Backend: Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB
  • Security: JWT authentication, bcrypt password hashing
  • API: RESTful API with Swagger documentation

🙏 Our Philosophy

Yoga is more than just physical exercise—it's a holistic practice that nurtures body, mind, and spirit. At Yoga 3D, we believe technology should enhance, not replace, the personal journey of yoga. Our platform is designed to support your practice, help you learn proper form, and track your progress while respecting the ancient traditions and wisdom of yoga.
